Exercises

Exercise 1

Transform the following sentences into passive voice:

  1. She writes a letter.
  2. The chef cooked a delicious meal.
  3. They will finish the project.
  4. We are watching a movie.
  5. The company will launch a new product.

Exercise 2

Complete the sentences in passive voice:

  1. The song (sing) by the artist.
  2. The report (finish) tomorrow.
  3. The cake (eat) by the kids.
  4. The documents (sign) by the manager.
  5. The new movie (release) next month.

Writing

Exercise 1

Write a short paragraph using passive voice about an important historical event.

Reading

Exercise 1

Read the following passage and identify the sentences in passive voice:

"The Declaration of Independence was signed in 1776. It was written by Thomas Jefferson. The document was approved by the Continental Congress."

Exercise 2

Read the following passage and answer the questions:

"The Mona Lisa, a famous painting by Leonardo da Vinci, was completed in 1506. It is known for its mysterious smile. The painting was stolen in 1911 and recovered in 1913."

  1. When was the Mona Lisa completed?
  2. What is the Mona Lisa known for?
  3. When was the painting stolen and recovered?
