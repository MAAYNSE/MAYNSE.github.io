Final Test

Section A: Sentence Transformation

Transform the following sentences into passive voice:

  1. "She speaks Spanish fluently."
  2. "The company awarded him a promotion."
  3. "We will finish the project by next Friday."
  4. "The teacher is explaining the lesson right now."
  5. "They have built a new bridge in our town."

Section B: Sentence Completion

Complete the following sentences in passive voice:

  1. The letters (send) yesterday.
  2. This novel (write) by a famous author.
  3. These old houses (demolish) next year.
  4. The contract (sign) by the CEO tomorrow.
  5. English (teach) to us by Mrs. Smith.

Section C: Reading Comprehension

Read the following passage and answer the questions:

"The Eiffel Tower, one of the most iconic landmarks in Paris, was designed by engineer Gustave Eiffel. It was completed in 1889 as the entrance arch to the 1889 World's Fair. Today, it is visited by millions of tourists every year."

  1. Who designed the Eiffel Tower?
  2. When was the Eiffel Tower completed?
  3. Why was the Eiffel Tower built originally?
