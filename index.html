PASSIVE VOICE

Meet us Description Grammar Examples

Description

The passive voice is used in English to focus attention on the action or the object receiving the action, rather than on the subject performing the action. It is formed with the verb 'to be' and the past participle of the main verb.

When is passive voice used?

Passive voice is used when the subject of the sentence is an object or thing that is acted upon. For example, consider a pizza. A pizza (as the subject of a sentence) can be expensive, taste good, have many ingredients, etc. However, there is a limit to the sentences we can make with a pizza as a subject, since it is still an object that does not perform actions itself. That's when the passive voice comes into play.

Grammar

The basic structure of a passive voice sentence is:

Subject + Verb 'to be' (in the appropriate tense) + Past participle of the main verb + complement.

Example: The cake was eaten (by John).

Time Active Voice Passive Voice
Present simple He bakes a pizza. A pizza is baked by him.
Present continuous He is baking a pizza. A pizza is being baked by him.
Past simple He baked a pizza. A pizza was baked by him.
Past continuous He was baking a pizza. A pizza was being baked by him.
Present perfect He has baked a pizza. A pizza has been baked by him.
Past perfect He had baked a pizza. A pizza had been baked by him.
Future simple (will) He will bake a pizza. A pizza will be baked by him.
Future with going to He is going to bake a pizza. A pizza is going to be baked by him.
Modal verbs (can, could, shall, should, would, may, must, might) He can bake a pizza. A pizza can be baked by him.
Perfect modals He should have baked a pizza. A pizza should have been baked by him.

Examples

  • George Orwell wrote the book. -------- The book was written by George Orwell.
  • They clean the windows every week. -------- The windows are cleaned every week.
  • The mechanic will repair the car. -------- The car will be repaired by the mechanic.
  • The workers are painting the house. -------- The house is being painted by the workers.
  • The secretary sent the letters. -------- The letters were sent by the secretary.
  • The project is being managed by a new team. -------- The project is being managed by a new team.
